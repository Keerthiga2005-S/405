Understanding Circles

Explore the properties and types of circles

Basic Circle

A circle is a simple shape in geometry consisting of all points in a plane that are at a given distance from a fixed point, the center.

Circumference

The circumference of a circle is the distance around it, calculated using the formula C = 2πr, where r is the radius.

Area

The area of a circle is the space enclosed within its circumference, calculated using the formula A = πr², where r is the radius.
