Dog Breeds

Discover various dog breeds and their characteristics

Labrador Retriever

Labrador Retrievers are friendly, outgoing, and high-spirited companions. They are known for their intelligence and loyalty.

Bulldog

Bulldogs are known for their distinctive wrinkled face and muscular build. They are calm, courageous, and have a friendly demeanor.

Poodle

Poodles are highly intelligent and trainable. They come in various sizes and are known for their curly coats and graceful appearance.
